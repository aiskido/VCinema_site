Wonder Woman

Rating: 7.0
Date: 15.05.2017
Age: 16+
Directory: Patty Jenkins

Overview

On the island of Themyscira, hidden from prying eyes, lives a tribe of warlike Amazons, whose task is to protect humanity from Ares, the insidious god of war. The daughter of Queen Hippolyta, Diana, shows unprecedented potential during training and, when the time comes, she is preparing to take up the sword “Killer of the Gods” in order to deal a crushing blow to her main enemy. One day, pilot Steve Trevor accidentally lands on the island, destined to change Diana's plans for the future and open up a huge world outside the island for her...
